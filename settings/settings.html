Do you collect/sell my browsing history, location, etc?

We will never collect any data from you and since we wont have it, we will never sell it.

Does this extension exist in other browsers?

Currently the extension is only availible in all Chromium based browsers (Microsoft Edge, Brave, etc).

What permissions do you use, and why do you use them?

We use the following permissions:

"permissions": [ "activeTab", "tabs", "storage" ],
We use these permissions to improve your experience. The "activeTab", "tabs" and "storage" permissions allows us to display the toolbar. The "storage" permission also allows us to save your preferences to your Chrome Account so that you don't have to constantly set them.
